Exaptlife FAQs

How do international students choose a US college?

International students can choose US colleges based on academic programs, location, campus culture, and available support services. We provide guidance and resources to help make this important decision.

How does Expatlife support international students?

Expatlife offers comprehensive support including visa assistance, academic guidance, cultural integration programs, and ongoing mentorship throughout the student's academic journey.

How does Expatlife help with college applications?

We provide end-to-end application support, including document preparation, essay guidance, test preparation, and application submission assistance to multiple universities.

How does Expatlife help international students with their career development?

We offer career counseling, resume workshops, interview preparation, networking opportunities, and connections to potential employers through our extensive professional network.

What other services does Expatlife offer?

Additional services include housing assistance, airport pickup, orientation programs, English language support, and ongoing academic and personal support throughout the student's stay.
